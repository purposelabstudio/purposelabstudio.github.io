❤️‍🩹 BP Log — FAQ

Does BP Log measure my blood pressure?

No. BP Log is a companion app for your home blood pressure monitor (cuff device). You take a reading with your monitor and enter the numbers into the app. BP Log does not use your phone's sensors to measure blood pressure.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data stays on your phone. We don't have servers, accounts, or cloud storage. Your readings are stored locally in a database on your device.

Can I export my readings?

Yes. You can generate a PDF or CSV report from the app and share it via email, WhatsApp, or any other app. This is useful for sharing with your doctor.

What are the blood pressure categories?

BP Log uses the American Heart Association (AHA) 2017 guidelines: Normal (below 120/80), Elevated (120-129 / below 80), Stage 1 (130-139 / 80-89), Stage 2 (140+ / 90+), and Crisis (above 180/120).

What if I uninstall the app?

Your data will be lost. Since everything is stored locally, uninstalling the app deletes your readings. We recommend exporting a PDF or CSV backup regularly.

🌙 Hushly — FAQ

Does Hushly work offline?

Yes. All sounds are included in the app. No internet connection is needed to play them.

Will Hushly drain my battery?

Hushly is optimized for overnight use. It handles battery restrictions on Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and other Android phones. Use the sleep timer to stop playback automatically.

Can I mix multiple sounds?

Check the app for the latest mixing features. We're continuously improving Hushly based on parent feedback.

Is there a subscription?

No. Hushly is free. If we add premium features in the future, it will be a one-time purchase — never a subscription.

General

Are your apps really free?

Yes. Our apps are free with no ads. Optional premium features may be available as a one-time purchase. We will never add mandatory subscriptions or advertising.

Do you collect my personal data?

We collect minimal anonymous analytics (crash reports, basic usage patterns) to improve our apps. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data. See our privacy policies for details.
