Support

Need help? Find answers below or reach out to us directly.

📔 Folio — FAQ

Is my journal private?

Yes. All your entries are stored locally on your phone. We don't have servers, accounts, or cloud sync. Your journal never leaves your device unless you manually export it.

Can I lock the app?

Yes. Folio includes an app lock feature using your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint or face unlock) or PIN. Enable it in Settings.

Can I export or backup my journal?

Yes. You can export your entries as a PDF or plain text file and save them to your device or share via email, Drive, or any other app.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Your journal entries will be lost. Since everything is stored locally, uninstalling deletes your data. We recommend exporting a backup regularly.

Is Folio really free?

Yes. All core features — journal entries, mood tracking, habits, monthly reflections — are free forever. Optional themes and customization may be available as a one-time purchase.

💧 WaterWise — FAQ

Does WaterWise have ads?

No. WaterWise is completely ad-free. You'll never see ads on the logging screen, in notifications, or anywhere in the app.

How do reminders work?

You can set custom reminders throughout the day. WaterWise respects Do Not Disturb mode and works with battery optimization on all Android phones.

Does it work offline?

Yes. WaterWise works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required.

Can I customize my daily goal?

Yes. You can set your target water intake based on your body weight, activity level, and preferences. The app also supports custom cup sizes.

Is there a subscription?

No. WaterWise is free with no mandatory purchases. If we add premium features in the future, it will be a small one-time purchase — never a subscription.

❤️‍🩹 BP Log — FAQ

Does BP Log measure my blood pressure?

No. BP Log is a companion app for your home blood pressure monitor (cuff device). You take a reading with your monitor and enter the numbers into the app. BP Log does not use your phone's sensors to measure blood pressure.

Is my data stored online?

No. All your data stays on your phone. We don't have servers, accounts, or cloud storage. Your readings are stored locally in a database on your device.

Can I export my readings?

Yes. You can generate a PDF or CSV report from the app and share it via email, WhatsApp, or any other app. This is useful for sharing with your doctor.

What are the blood pressure categories?

BP Log uses the American Heart Association (AHA) 2017 guidelines: Normal (below 120/80), Elevated (120-129 / below 80), Stage 1 (130-139 / 80-89), Stage 2 (140+ / 90+), and Crisis (above 180/120).

What if I uninstall the app?

Your data will be lost. Since everything is stored locally, uninstalling the app deletes your readings. We recommend exporting a PDF or CSV backup regularly.

🌙 Hushly — FAQ

Does Hushly work offline?

Yes. All sounds are included in the app. No internet connection is needed to play them.

Will Hushly drain my battery?

Hushly is optimized for overnight use. It handles battery restrictions on Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and other Android phones. Use the sleep timer to stop playback automatically.

Can I mix multiple sounds?

Check the app for the latest mixing features. We're continuously improving Hushly based on parent feedback.

Is there a subscription?

No. Hushly is free. If we add premium features in the future, it will be a one-time purchase — never a subscription.
