Frequently Asked Questions

What is Crumbs?

Crumbs is a private second brain you talk to on WhatsApp. You dump anything you want to remember — notes, ideas, todos, reminders, links, photos, voice notes, contacts, locations — and ask for it back later in plain language. It's the calmer upgrade to messaging yourself.

Do I need to install an app?

No. Crumbs works entirely inside WhatsApp — no new app to learn. A dedicated Crumbs mobile app for Android and iOS is coming soon, where the same saved items are easier to browse and connect.

Is Crumbs private?

Yes. Your words stay yours. Sensitive content is encrypted, your data is portable, and Crumbs never fabricates a memory — recall is grounded only in what you actually saved. No third party trains on your content.

What languages does Crumbs understand?

Crumbs is multilingual and India-first. It understands English, Hindi, Hinglish and many other languages, so you can dump and recall in whatever way feels natural.

Who builds Crumbs?

Crumbs is built by PurposeLab Studio, the maker of simple, privacy-first apps like Folio, WaterWise, BP Log and Hushly.
