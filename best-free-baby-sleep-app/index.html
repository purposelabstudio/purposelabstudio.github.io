The Best Free Baby Sleep Sounds App (No Ads)

At 2am you want soothing sound in one tap — not an ad, a sign-up, or a paywall. Here's what actually matters in a baby sleep app, and the honest case for a calmer pick.

What to look for in a free baby sleep app

  • Curated soothing sounds — white noise, rain, lullabies that actually help.
  • One-handed controls — big, simple buttons for the middle of the night.
  • Sleep timer — fade out after your baby settles.
  • Background playback — keeps playing with the screen off.
  • Works offline — a dropped connection should never wake the baby.
  • No ads — nothing loud or bright interrupting sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free baby sleep sounds app?

One with curated soothing sounds, simple one-handed controls, a timer, background playback, offline support, and no ads. Hushly is built to do exactly that.

Is it safe to play sleep sounds all night?

A low, safe volume for the whole sleep period is fine — around 50 dB, with the device at least 2 metres from the crib. A fade-out timer works too.

Do baby sleep apps work offline?

The best ones play offline so a dropped connection never wakes your baby. Hushly works offline with no account.
