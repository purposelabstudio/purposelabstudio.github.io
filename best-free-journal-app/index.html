The Best Free Journal App With Lock (Private, Offline)

A journal is only useful if it's genuinely private and easy to keep up. Here's what actually matters — and the honest case for an offline, ad-free app with a lock.

What to look for in a free journal app

  • App lock — a PIN or biometric lock so entries stay private on a shared phone.
  • Offline & on-device — your diary shouldn't live on someone else's server.
  • Quick daily entry — low friction is what makes the habit stick.
  • Mood & habit tracking — see patterns alongside your words.
  • No ads — a calm, distraction-free space to write.
  • No account — nothing to sign up for.
