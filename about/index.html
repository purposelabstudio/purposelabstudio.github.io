About PurposeLab Studio

Simple apps that respect your time, money, and privacy.

Our Mission

Too many apps are built to extract — your attention, your data, your money. We believe everyday tools should be simple, honest, and respectful.

PurposeLab Studio builds Android apps for real problems. We focus on things people need every day — tracking health, helping babies sleep, staying connected with family. Each app does one thing, does it well, and stays out of your way.

We're an indie studio based in India, building for Indian users first and the world second.

Our Values

🚫

No Ads

We don't show ads. Your experience is the product, not your attention.

🔒

Privacy-First

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, sell, or share personal information.

💰

Honest Pricing

Free apps are truly free. If we add premium features, it's a one-time purchase — never a subscription.

🎯

Do One Thing Well

Each app solves one problem. No feature bloat, no unnecessary complexity.
