About PurposeLab Studio

Simple apps that respect your time, money, and privacy.

Our Mission

Too many apps are built to extract — your attention, your data, your money. We believe everyday tools should be simple, honest, and respectful.

PurposeLab Studio builds Android apps for real problems. We focus on things people need every day — journaling, hydration, health tracking, baby care. Each app does one thing, does it well, and stays out of your way.

We're an indie studio based in India, building for Indian users first and the world second.

Meet the Founder

PurposeLab Studio was founded by Atul Chaturvedi, the developer behind every app here. After years of watching everyday apps fill up with ads, trackers, and subscriptions, he started PurposeLab to build the opposite — small, private, ad-free tools that do one useful thing and stay out of your way.

He designs, builds, and writes for PurposeLab from India, and reads every message that comes in. Say hello on LinkedIn or email purposelab.studio@gmail.com.
