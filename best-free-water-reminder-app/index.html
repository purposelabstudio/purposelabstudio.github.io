The Best Free Water Reminder App (No Ads)

A water reminder should do one thing well: nudge you to drink and log it in a tap. Here's what actually matters — and the honest case for an ad-free pick with no paywall.

What to look for in a free water app

  • One-tap logging — record a glass without digging through menus.
  • Smart reminders — gentle nudges you can tune to your day, not spam.
  • Streaks & progress — a simple way to stay motivated.
  • Works offline — logging and reminders shouldn't need a connection.
  • No ads — nothing between you and a quick log.
  • No paywall — the core habit shouldn't cost money.
