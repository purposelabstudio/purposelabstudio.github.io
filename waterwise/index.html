Frequently Asked Questions

Is WaterWise free?

Yes. WaterWise is completely free with no ads, no subscriptions, and no paywall. All features are available from day one.

How much water should I drink daily?

The general recommendation is about 2.7 liters (women) to 3.7 liters (men) per day from all sources. WaterWise calculates a personalized goal based on your weight, activity, and climate.

Does WaterWise need an internet connection?

No. WaterWise works completely offline. All data is stored locally. No account or sign-up required.

Does WaterWise have a widget?

Yes. A home screen widget shows your daily progress and lets you log water with a single tap without opening the app.

How do reminders work?

Smart reminders throughout your waking hours at intervals you choose. They pause during sleep time and stop once you hit your daily goal.

Does WaterWise collect my data?

No. All data is stored locally on your phone only. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no personal data collection.

What are hydration streaks?

Streaks track consecutive days you've hit your water goal. They provide motivation to build a consistent hydration habit.
